For Customers:

Online Vehicle Purchase Platform:
Provide an online platform where customers can browse the available vehicles.
Allow customers to customize vehicle options (e.g., color, features).
Enable customers to submit purchase requests directly through the platform.

Purchase Management:
Offer a feature where customers can access their purchase history.
Enable customers to view detailed information about their previous vehicle purchases.
Allow customers to manage their purchases, including tracking payment statuses and delivery updates.


For Vehicle Sales Personnel:

Customer Inquiry Management:
Provide a system that clearly and accurately communicates customer inquiries to sales personnel.
Ensure that inquiries are handled efficiently, enabling timely responses and follow-up.

Purchase Handling:
Provide real-time updates on new vehicle purchases.
Track payment statuses and vehicle arrival for effective sales management.
Allow sales personnel to monitor the status of vehicle purchases and engage with customers as necessary to ensure satisfaction.
