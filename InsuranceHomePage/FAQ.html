Insurance FAQs

1. What is insurance?
Insurance is a risk management tool that helps protect individuals or businesses from potential financial losses by paying a premium.
2. Why do I need insurance?
Insurance can help alleviate financial stress during unexpected events, ensuring your financial security.
3. How is insurance premium calculated?
Insurance premiums are typically calculated based on various factors, including the amount of coverage, type of insurance, your age, and health status.
4. Can I use my insurance anywhere?
This depends on your insurance policy. Some coverage may only be valid in specific areas or under certain circumstances.
5. How do I file a claim?
You can usually file a claim through your insurance company's website, by phone, or in person. Be prepared with relevant documents and information.
